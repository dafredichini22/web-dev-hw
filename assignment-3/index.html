Laura Bush's Cowboy Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 sticks (12 ounces) butter, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups packed light brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 3 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 3 cups old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 2 cups sweetened flaked coconut
  • 2 cups chopped pecans

Directions

  1. Position oven racks in the upper and lower thirds and preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon and salt.
  4. Using a stand mixer on medium speed, beat butter until smooth and creamy, about 1 min.
  5. Gradually add sugars and beat to combine, about 2 min.
  6. Add eggs, one at a time, beating after each one, and the vanilla extract.
  7. Reduce speed to low and add flour mixture gradually until just combined.
  8. Add the chocolate chips, oats, coconut and pecans, mixing until just combined.

For Large Cookies

  1. Drop a hefty 1/4 cup of dough onto the baking sheets for each cookie, spacing 3 inches apart.
  2. Bake for 8 to 9 min. , then rotate sheets top to bottom and front to back.
  3. Bake for an additional 8 to 9 min. or until edges are lightly browned.
  4. Let cookies cool on baking sheet for about 5 min. , then transfer to rack to cool completely.
  5. Store in airtight containers at room temperature for several days. May be frozen.

For Smaller Cookies

  1. Use 2 Tbs. dough per cookie.
  2. Bake for 15 to 18 min. total, rotating the baking sheets midway through.
  3. Let cookies cool on baking sheet for about 5 min. , then transfer to rack to cool completely.
  4. Store in airtight containers at room temperature for several days. May be frozen.
